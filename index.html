About Me

An experienced IT professional, developer, architect and project manager, including leadership and CSM roles. Hands-on full stack developer and database designer, evolved to architect where I shepherded engagements through the full lifecycle of a project: from concept definition, solution architecture, to ‘go-live’, including operations and maintenance. In addition, I have developed extensive project management skills, whether collaborating with peer project managers and the PMO or serving as the senior project manager on an engagement. I have constructed and presented oral presentations to assist client senior executives and stakeholders to gain consensus on the expectations for each project. I passionately embraced challenges such those engagements which demanded that I quickly develop business acumen across a variety of applications and processes that are new to me and how to improve the business processes leveraging technology.
